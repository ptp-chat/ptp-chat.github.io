An open letter to previous members of PTP and guests.

As many of you have noticed, PTP's discord no longer exists and you were told to move to the continously growing Programmers server. I made this decision because I felt that PTP was not growing or becoming better it continued to remain a closely knit group with hardly any collaboration or programming related discussion. In fact a majority of the conversation was very general and or completely unrelated.

I created PTP to provide a community where developers could come and talk to other developers, meet, collaborate and discuss. The way PTP was operating was contrary to that and I felt that it would be best to move everyone to an environment where they could actually achieve this.

Looking towards the future I see a much better outcome for the previous members of PTP. The Programmers server provides an environment where you are exposed to developers with more experience, you can create more connections and friends, and most importantly it is an active community.

I apologize for the connections that you have lost or for those of you who were considering joining, the connections that you may of lost. However I felt this was a needed move and I regret nothing.

Thank you, Sean Wilson.
