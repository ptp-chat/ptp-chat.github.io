Join PTP Make sure to read the rules!

PTP (People That Program) is a group dedicated to providing developers and programmers with a general chat to share their ideas, receive feedback on concepts or just chat about programming in general.

Rules

  1. No spamming
  2. Consult with administrators before adding a bot
  3. Keep messages in English
  4. Keep the overall topic on development & programming
  5. Do not re-add or invite members that have left or been removed
  6. Do not cause needless notifications (unless its important)
  7. Do not advertise commercial services
  8. Do not use any racial slurs, or derivitives of such
  9. Do not post personal information (addresses, etc) about others without their consent
  10. Use common sense

General Information

  1. If you would like to add a bot, contact one of the managers or owners
